About Us

Tech Enthusiasm Liberia is a growing community of passionate learners who are building strong foundations in technology. We believe that learning together, supporting one another, and practicing consistently can turn beginners into confident builders. Our focus is growth, collaboration, and real-world learning.

Vision

To empower young people in Liberia through technology, innovation, and collaboration.

Mission

To create a supportive learning environment where beginners grow their skills in web development and technology through teamwork and practice.
